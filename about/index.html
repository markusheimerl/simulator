Experience Our Drone Simulator

Want to see our drones in action? Check out our interactive drone simulator! Here's how to get started:

  1. Enter your OpenAI API key in the input field and click the checkmark button. This will enable the chat interface.
  2. In the chat, give your drone an objective or task to perform within the simulated environment.
  3. Watch as the drone autonomously navigates the environment based on your instructions! The drone's AI will analyze its surroundings using the video feed and make decisions on how to best complete its objective.
  4. You can continue to chat with the drone, giving it new commands or refining its goals. The drone will use the conversation history to inform its actions.
  5. Use the WASD keys to attach and detach the camera from the drone. While detached, you can freely move and look around the environment using the WASD keys and mouse.

This simulator provides a glimpse into the incredible capabilities of our autonomous drones.
