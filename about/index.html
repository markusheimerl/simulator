Engage with Our Drone Simulator

Our interactive simulator offers a hands-on experience with our autonomous drones, allowing users to witness firsthand the sophistication and capabilities of our technology. Here’s how to participate:

  1. Enter your OpenAI API key to activate the chat interface, enabling real-time interaction with the drone.
  2. Command the drone to execute tasks within the simulated environment, observing its autonomous navigation and decision-making processes.
  3. The drone will dynamically interact with its surroundings, processing data and executing tasks based on real-time environmental analysis.
  4. You can adjust the drone's objectives or issue new commands as the simulation progresses, which allows for a responsive and interactive user experience.
  5. Control the camera’s perspective using the WASD keys for a comprehensive overview or a focused view of the drone's environment.

This simulator not only demonstrates the advanced capabilities of our drones but also provides insight into their potential real-world applications, from infrastructure inspection to emergency response scenarios.
